Roasted Mushrooms

Description

A simple yet delicious recipe, roasted mushrooms pair well with many meat and vegetable dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 pounds white button or cremini mushrooms, cleaned and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 sprigs fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Toss mushrooms with olive oil in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Transfer mushrooms to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Scatter thyme sprigs evenly over the top.
  4. Roast until the mushrooms are well browned and flavorful, 30 to 45 minutes.
  5. Discard the thyme sprigs, transfer the mushrooms to a serving bowl, and serve.
